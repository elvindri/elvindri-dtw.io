About Pelita Harapan University

One university that can be the right choice is Pelita Harapan University (UPH). UPH is a Private Higher Education Institution (PTS) with 13 faculty choices that have proven quality. Established in 1994, UPH has more than 50,000 graduates who have worked in various fields. UPH's excellence has also been proven by UPH being selected as one of the best PTS 2022 according to the QS Asia University Rankings. A multitude of achievements have been proven by UPH students, alumni, and lecturers, both on a national and international scale, including winning the Asia Young Designer of the Year title, at the 2020 Asia Young Designer Awards (AYDA), 1st place in the 2022 Philip C. Jessup International Law Moot Court Competition (ILMCC), and others.

Source: www.uph.edu
